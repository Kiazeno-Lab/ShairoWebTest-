A biography composed in pixels and presence

I grew up curious — sketchbook in one hand, command line in the other. Today, I design and build experiences that make people feel seen, supported, and inspired. My process is part ritual, part research: listen deeply, define clearly, iterate kindly, and ship with care.

Off screen, you’ll find me chasing light with a camera, cooking for friends, and learning from the quiet wisdom of long walks. These moments outside the editor nourish the work within it — they remind me that technology is at its best when it brings us back to our humanity.

— Your Name
